Eco-Friendly Cars Guide
This environmentally friendly automobile (a.k.a. The green automobile, or eco friendly car) is a vehicle that emits down carbon compounds when it works. This flow of environmentally friendly automobiles makes this situation safer because changes in climate and health risks that lead from the inhalation of carbon compounds are greatly reduced. Aside from being environmentally friendly, green automobiles also have the benefit of keeping money since that alternate fuels they take do not consume as fast as the traditional types.

Vehicles that operate primarily on gasoline or diesel have historically accounted for over 99% of cars and passenger truck sales (EPA.gov). Cars that operate on an alternative method rather than gas or diesel, particularly electricity are growing and are the future. New models of electric and hybrid vehicles are increasing in the market and production annually. Alternatives such as electric and hybrid cars operate and release cleaner emissions than gasoline and diesel. Reducing vehicle emissions and increasing fuel economy will not only benefit the environment, but also your money!

Fueling
Fueling an electric or hybrid car is quite simple. If you have a charging station in your garage at home then simply plug it into your car. Otherwise, there are plenty of fueling stations available and the numbers are still growing.
